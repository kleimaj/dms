DMS is proud to be a diverse workplace embracing three core values: honesty, integrity and respect. Our focus every day is to support the health and happiness of our employees. Here are some of the ways we do this:

DMS has a variety of work schedules available, depending on your position and location. Also, certain positions are structured so employees can work from home. Learn more about which schedules are available for what jobs when you apply.

No matter where you are in your life, DMS can offer you a place to learn something new, hone your current skills and maybe even find your passion. We offer leadership development, on-the-job training and a new cross-training program in our production area.

At DMS, we believe we can collectively make a positive impact on our local community and beyond. Our Charitable Giving Committee coordinates events for all employees to give to others. We maintain a comprehensive recycling program and are always keeping an eye out for other ways to conserve and preserve our world.

At DMS, safety is every employee’s #1 priority. We are committed to creating and supporting a SAFETY-FIRST work environment for all employees, and we do everything possible to prevent workplace injuries and accidents.

Whether it’s a cool DMS hydrating water bottle, a Walking Club pedometer, or a fresh apple to chomp on, we offer a new incentive each month to help our employees stay happy and healthy!

We offer a variety of benefits to our employees and their families including:

  • Flexible Spending Accounts for Medical Expenses & Dependent Care
  • Paid Vacation & Sick Time
  • Life Insurance & Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D)
  • Supplemental Medical & Income Replacement Coverage
  • Paid Holidays
  • 401k Retirement Savings Plan
  • Long & Short-Term Disability
