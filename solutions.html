WHAT WE OFFER

Use any one or every one of our services; you’ll find us equally talented in all.

As an integrated direct marketing leader and innovator, DMS creates agile, response-driven solutions based on data-focused strategies customized for each client’s needs and challenges. Whether your prospect is offline or online, we make sure your brand reaches them. Our tools have evolved to marry best practices with modern technology, so every campaign is strategic, measurable, and scalable.

We embrace your needs and goals, then develop customized strategies to exceed them. Based on marketing metrics and key performance indicators, we collaboratively and proactively make recommendations that will optimize: 1) testing, 2) channel selection, 3) data selects, 4) tempo, and 5) creative (for offline and online channels). DMS will streamline the entire process. Our goal is to create an attribution scenario that helps bring order and opportunity to chaos while improving your marketing ROI.

We’ve built an industry-leading data analytics team rather than a list of outside service providers. As the adage goes: “If you want something done right, do it yourself.” Whether it’s data processing, modeling or everything in between, we’ll build you a customized solution tailored to your individual business needs. Our data and analytics experts are unparalleled in the industry and will work to ensure your campaign’s success.

Our world-class marketing agency is one of the best in the business. Our creative team consists of channel-agnostic, integrated direct marketing experts — ready to maximize your campaign’s success with a powerful combination of new technology and time-tested methodology. With a direct marketing mindset, they can help build your brand from the ground up or work within existing guidelines to reach your goals. They think strategically because they know good creative is creative that sells. Adroit and nimble, each team member excels at their craft to deploy effective creative concepts that drive your brand.

We bring our direct response experience, reasoning and strategies to the table and embed them in our digital marketing executions. We recognize technology as a valuable direct marketing tool in this progressive world. That’s why we diligently monitor industry trends and look for new ways to innovate. As part of our integrated approach, we use marketing attribution to identify and optimize the best performing channels to achieve your goals and ensure they work together seamlessly. Whether it’s SEO, PPC/SEM, Social, IP Targeting or any other channel available, our team of digital pros can optimize a plan that makes the most sense for your bottom line.

Our Account Managers are the bridge between your challenges and our solutions. They manage all facets of your campaigns while coordinating with each pertinent DMS department to ensure your project gets the attention it deserves. As direct marketing experts and your single point of contact, they are your advocates and part of your team. Our AMs get to know your unique wants, needs and expectations to achieve a symbiotic and intuitive relationship.

All our direct mail production solutions are achieved by a team of client-focused professionals who are flexible to your specific needs and ensure top quality. Between DMS West & DMS East, we can deploy over one hundred million pieces monthly. With diverse production equipment, combined with our skilled hand-assembly team, we have the ability to produce a wide variety of sizes and formats. Your job will be printed, personalized, inserted and mailed all from within our secure, SOC 2 compliant facilities. Want it fast? No problem, we’re used to quick turns. Workflow control is one of the benefits of keeping things in-house.

Clients can keep an eye on ROI with DMS’ proprietary platform CampaignMinderTM. This versatile tool connects all the pieces of your integrated marketing efforts, allowing you to review and revise them from a single dashboard. From real-time attribution data to collaborative creative editing, learn more about the benefits of CampaignMinderTM here.
